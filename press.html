About Binder
Key facts and figures for journalists.

Binder is India's premium photobook platform. We enable anyone to design a professional-quality photobook in their browser and have it printed and delivered anywhere in India.

Our studio is based in Okhla Phase I, New Delhi. We print at 300 DPI in CMYK on 200gsm premium matte paper, with same-week turnaround and nationwide delivery.

The platform is available at binder.co.in and integrates with Shopify for a seamless ordering experience.
