Whether you're preserving a holiday, a wedding, or just a year of everyday moments — the size of your photobook shapes how your story feels in someone's hands.

## The 6×6" — intimate and personal

Small enough to fit on any shelf, the 6×6" is perfect for a single event, a personal gift, or a short series. It feels precious rather than grand — the kind of book you pick up and flick through quietly.

## The 8×8" — the sweet spot

Our most popular size for a reason. Big enough for impact, small enough to keep on a coffee table. Works beautifully for weddings, travel albums, and family annuals.

> "The 8×8\" just feels right in your hands. Not too big, not too small — it's exactly the right canvas for a life well-documented."

## The 12×12" — a statement piece

When you want to go big. Full-bleed photos fill the page edge to edge. The 12×12" is a coffee-table book in the truest sense — it demands to be seen.
